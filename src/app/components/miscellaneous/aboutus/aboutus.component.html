  • Website that allows users to sign up and login to browse and/or book from a catalogue of hotels.
  • Search bar on the home page allows the user to search hotels by city (eg. Gurugram, Noida or Chandigarh).
  • Once logged in the user can book a hotel by selecting a from-date and to-date and number of rooms on the hotel details page. Used Reactive Forms to update total amount as the user selects these options.
  • Booking can then be completed by making a payment on the checkout page.
  • Users can also view their previous bookings on the bookings page.
